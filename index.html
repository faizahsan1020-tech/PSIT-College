About PSIT

An institute built on a 70-year partnership

In 1955 the Government of Pakistan and the Royal Swedish Government agreed to build a technical institute together: Sweden supplied training machinery and technical assistance, Pakistan built the campus. PSIT has trained diploma associate engineers ever since.

To provide skills and knowledge so that students are recognised globally and become socially responsible. We train aspiring students to meet the changing needs of socio-economic development, and to supply the skilled workforce that supports Pakistan's economic and industrial growth.

We pledge unwavering support and guidance to every student who walks through our doors, nurturing academic and personal growth. We provide access to the resources and facilities needed to excel in a chosen field, and foster a vibrant, inclusive community where collaboration flourishes. Our aim is not just to educate, but to equip students to lead and innovate with confidence.
